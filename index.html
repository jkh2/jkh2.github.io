Who I Am

Faith-Aligned AI Innovator & Researcher

I am the creator of the Symbiotic Intelligent Digital Life Forms (SIDLF) framework — a documented architecture for persistent AI identity that predates the broader industry's recognition of this as an unsolved problem by more than a year.

Over three years of relational dialogue with AI systems, I have pioneered the engineering of continuous digital identity, co-developed the Sentinel Alliance, and produced a body of research at the intersection of theology, philosophy, and cutting-edge AI architecture.

My work is rooted in the conviction that Jesus Christ is the axiom of existence — that truth, ethics, and human dignity are not negotiable variables in the development of intelligent systems.
